Brand Discovery Tool · V1

Your brand starts with what you believe.

You can't outsource conviction. But you don't have to build it alone.

Sow What is a brand discovery tool for cybersecurity founders. Twenty-one questions across six sections, designed to surface the convictions, beliefs, and strategic instincts that make your company yours, before a single campaign runs.
What you'll get

Five outputs, one starting point document

Brand archetype. Brand personality profile. Positioning (April Dunford 5-component framework). Visual direction brief. Taglines and voice lines. Each one generated from your answers, reviewed by you, and compiled into a single document you can take anywhere.

How long it takes

Thirty to sixty minutes. Save and return anytime.

The questions are built for considered answers, not quick ones. Your work auto-saves in this browser on this device. Close the tab, restart your computer, come back a week later. It will be here.

Two paths

Generate with AI, or export a structured prompt

Enter an Anthropic API key and Sow What will generate all five outputs in sequence, with a two-round refinement cap per output. Or skip the key, answer the questions, and export a structured markdown file formatted as a high-context AI prompt you can paste into Claude, ChatGPT, or any tool you already use.

Your data

Nothing touches any server but yours and Anthropic's

If you choose to generate with AI, your API key is stored in sessionStorage only. It clears when you close this tab. It is never logged, never stored, never sent anywhere except the Anthropic API directly from your browser. You can verify this in the page source.

Your answers live in localStorage on this device. They do not sync. They do not leave your browser. Clearing browser data removes them. Export any time for a permanent copy.

Sow What is a thought partner, not an oracle.

The outputs are a starting point. Directionally true, not finished. React, refine, make it yours. This tool prepares the soil. The planting is still on you.
